Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They store information about your visit and are used to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our website.

Why We Use Cookies

Carrier Connect uses cookies for various purposes:

  • Authentication: To remember your login information and keep you logged in.
  • Preferences: To remember your settings, language, and layout preferences.
  • Analytics: To track visitor behavior and understand how users interact with our website.
  • Performance: To optimize website performance and load times.
  • Security: To prevent fraud and enhance security features.
  • Advertising: To show relevant job postings and personalized content.

Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies

Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. We use these to maintain your browsing session and keep you logged in while using our platform.

Persistent Cookies

Persistent cookies remain on your device for a longer period or until you delete them. We use these to remember your preferences and improve your experience on subsequent visits.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and other analytics tools that place their own cookies on your device. These help us understand how visitors use our site and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They include:

  • Authentication tokens
  • Security tokens
  • Load balancing session identifiers

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to collect information about how you use our website, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Links clicked
  • Search queries performed
  • Device and browser information
  • Geographic location

Marketing Cookies

These cookies help us deliver targeted advertising and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns. They may track your browsing across different websites to show you relevant job opportunities.

Cookie Management

Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Accept or reject cookies
  • Delete cookies at any time
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Disabling Cookies

While you can disable cookies in your browser settings, please note that doing so may:

  • Prevent you from logging into your account
  • Disable certain features on our website
  • Reduce the personalization of your experience
  • Affect how our website performs

Opt-Out Options

To opt out of personalized advertising based on your browsing behavior, you can:

  • Visit the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page
  • Use your browser's "Do Not Track" feature
  • Update your preferences in your account settings

Specific Cookies We Use

User Session Cookie

  • Name: sessionid
  • Type: Session
  • Purpose: Maintains user session and authentication
  • Duration: Until browser closes

Google Analytics Cookie

  • Name: _ga
  • Type: Persistent
  • Purpose: Tracks visitor behavior for analytics
  • Duration: 2 years

Remember Me Cookie

  • Name: remember_me
  • Type: Persistent
  • Purpose: Remembers your login preferences
  • Duration: 30 days

Preference Cookie

  • Name: user_preferences
  • Type: Persistent
  • Purpose: Stores your theme and layout preferences
  • Duration: 1 year

Data Security

We protect cookie data using industry-standard encryption and security measures. Sensitive information such as passwords and payment details are never stored in cookies.

Third-Party Services

We work with third-party service providers who may set cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and user behavior tracking
  • Facebook Pixel: For remarketing and campaign optimization
  • LinkedIn Insights: For professional networking analytics
  • Hotjar: For user session recordings and heatmaps

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies. We recommend reviewing them to understand how they use cookies.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at support@jobportal.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Your continued use of our website constitutes your acceptance of the updated Cookie Policy.
